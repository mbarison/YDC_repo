Sponsors

We are pleased to have had support from many generous donors throughout our existence. We are particularly grateful to the Unitarian Church of Montreal which has provided us with a home since 1998.

For more information on becoming a sponsor of the Yellow Door Choir, please contact us.

Message from the Unitarian Church

The Yellow Door Choir has had a long association with the Unitarian Church of Montreal, rehearsing in the hall for the past ten years or more, holding many concerts in the sanctuary, and, from time to time, gracing our Sunday services with song. The ideals of music, culture, and support of worthy community groups have been shared by the Unitarian Church and The Yellow Door Choir during all those years. We are pleased to support their mission of building community through their performances and fostering an understanding of the rich cultural heritage of Montreal with their varied repertoire. Alison Hall, President, Unitarian Church of Montreal (2008) ucmtl.ca

Become a Friend of the Yellow Door Choir

For a gift of $25 or more you can become a Friend of the Yellow Door Choir. Support from our Friends enables us to continue to share the gift of song and also to help community organizations in their fundraising efforts.

As a Friend, you will have your name listed in our program book and will receive advance e-mails about our upcoming concerts.

Those of you who have already become Friends, please accept our heartfelt appreciation.
