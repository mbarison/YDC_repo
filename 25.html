25th Anniversary

The Yellow Door Choir celebrated its 25th anniversary on January 26, 2008 with a sold-out gala concert at Oscar Peterson Hall in Montreal. The evening featured guest host Jeanette Kelly of CBC radio, 65 alumni, past accompanists, past conductors (such as Linda Morrison), 570 fans and friends, as well as powerful musical memories.
