Membership Responsibilities

As a member of the Yellow Door Choir, you are required to:

  1. Attend all concerts (the concert year usually completes in the first week of June).
  2. Attend rehearsals and sectionals.
  3. Learn the words and music, and practice at home.
  4. Contribute some other skill to the choir (such as marketing and ticket sales) as we all share in the administrative and creative running of the choir.
